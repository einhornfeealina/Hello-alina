Alina Schroeder

Works at the intersection of interactive systems, design research, and storytelling — in the digital, in space, sometimes in both at once. Her projects begin with real questions: How does war continue to shape us across generations? How do you make climate change felt without overwhelming? How does an AI learn — and what does that say about us? What remains when things go untold? For her, design is a way of making complexity not explainable, but experienceable.
